Say to Alexa

Assume you named your TV in ihc App as “TV”, you can use these commands to control it:

Power control Alexa, turn on TV
Alexa, turn off TV
Alexa, power on TV
Alexa, power on TV
Volumn control Alexa, turn up volume by 5 on TV
Alexa, volume up on TV
Alexa, volume down on TV
Alexa, turn up the volume on TV
Alexa, turn down the volume on TV
Alexa, mute on TV
Alexa, unmute on TV
Channel control Alexa, channel up on TV
Alexa, channel down on TV
Alexa, change channel to 30 on TV
Alexa, change channel to FOX on TV *
Multimedia control Alexa, play on TV
Alexa, stop on TV
Alexa, resume on TV
Alexa, next on TV
Alexa, next song on TV
Alexa, set rewind on TV
Alexa, set fast forward on TV

* you need channel setup before using this command
