Download and Install App

If you don't know how to download ihc app from the App Store, please watch the video.

If you don't know how to download ihc app by scan the QR code on package, please watch the video.

Download ihc app from the App Store or Google Play by searching “Intelligent Home Center” or scan the QR code on package.

The app supports iOS 8.0 / Android 4.0 and above.

Then, connect to your Wi-Fi network which has internet access.

Note:

1) The Universal Remote only supports Wi-Fi network of 2.4GHz.

2) The length of password should be no more than 32 characters.
