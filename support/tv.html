Add your TV and STB

If you don't know how to add TV or STB, please watch the video.

If it is in first time use, the app will automatically show the type of appliances for you to add a remote. Select the type of the appliances you want to control and add the remote. Take TV as example, if your TV is digital TV (without STB), then tap “TV”, select the brand of TV.

Then, turn off your TV using physical remote and follow the instructions to start the matching test. During the test, the universal remote will try to send several control commands to the appliance and what you need to do is only to confirm the results. When the test is done, the app will show the remote panel with all buttons mapped to the physical remote.

If you learn the button by mistake, please tap “…” on the top right of the corner, then tap “Learn” to re-learn the button. Fox example, if VOL + is wrong, tap “Learn again” on the right of the button of “VOL+”, the indicator LED will turn orange. Press “VOL+” on the physical remote toward RM pro, the orange indicator LED will be off, and the button will be re-learn successfully.

If your TV is with STB, then please learn the TV remote first according to above steps, as you need to turn on/off TV before using STB. Then learn the remote of STB: Tap RM pro in the room, then tap “+”, select “STB”, choose the brand of your STB. Then learn the remote of STB, steps similar with TV remote learning.

If you cannot find the brand in the list, then you can use “User-defined remote” to learn the button one by one. Tap “+”, the indicator LED will turn orange, please point your remote to RM pro and press the button on physical remote to learn, the orange LED will be off, and the button will be learnt successfully. Name the button and select an icon for it.

If you would like to voice control the channel by channel name, Tap “CH” in the remote panel, then input the channel name and channel No., then save it. (Only when you complete the account link steps and discover the device in the Alexa App, you can voice control the channel by its name, please go to section III to learn it.)
