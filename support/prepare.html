Prepare Device

Power on the device with USB cable. If it is in first time use, the blue Wi-Fi LED will flash quickly (about 2-3 times per second) indicating the device is in configuration mode (waiting to be added into network).

If the device is not in first time use, it may not work in configuration mode automatically. In this case, please reset it by pressing and holding Reset Button with a slim needle for not less than 6s until the blue Wi-Fi LED flashes quickly.
