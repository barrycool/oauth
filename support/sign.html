Sign Up Your Account

If you don't know how to sign in with your email address, please watch the video.

If you don't know how to sign in with your Facebook credential, please watch the video.

Sign up for ihc account:

If you sign up with your email address, we will send you a verification code to this email. Please check your email and input the code and set your password to finish account setup. If you sign up with your cell phone number, the verification code will be sent to your phone as SMS.

Note:

If you did not receive the verification code, please check your spam mail. If you still cannot receive it, please try again with other email address. If you use phone number to sign up, please check if your cellular network is fine and you may need to check if the SMS is blocked by certain Apps.

If you have a Facebook account, you can also sign in with your Facebook credential.
