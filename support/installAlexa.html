Install Alexa

Install Alexa App, then log in by your Amazon account. (If you don’t have account, please create one). Tap the menu button and then tap “Settings”. Then tap “SET UP A NEW DEVICE” then choose a device to set up. Then, choose language of Alexa.

Long press the power button of Alexa till the light ring turn to orange, and Alexa will tell you then she is in setup mode. Then connect your smart phone to Alexa’s Wi-Fi (Wi-Fi name Amazon-XXX), wait for a white, Alexa will tell you she is connected. Then connect your smart phone’s Wi-Fi back to your home’s Wi-Fi, then Alexa will take a few minutes to complete setup.
